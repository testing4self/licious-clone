About Licious Kebabs

Kebab, derived from the Arabic word Cabob, means burn or char. Having originated in the Middle Eastern kitchens of Persia and Turkey, a kebab is made from marinated ground meat that is charcoal-grilled or skewered on fire. Kebabs have been one of the most popular delicacies in royal cuisine, be it for a royal gathering, festival or any other feast.

Kebab is a versatile dish usually made from lamb, goat or chicken meat. There are many varieties when it comes to kebab - shami kebab, galouti kebab, reshami kebab or seekh kebab to name a few. Over the years, kebab has become a symbol of fine dining; however, you can now enjoy delicious kebab starters in the comfort of your home too. These are easy to prepare in just two steps - Marinate and Grill.

At Licious, we make the process even simpler for you. We provide you with the best quality chicken and mutton kebabs that are effortless to cook. All you have to do is take them out on a plate or a pan and cook them as required. As we are very particular about the food that we serve you, our in-house chefs prepare these kebabs diligently. The chefs follow original gourmet recipes to prepare these kebabs using fresh chicken or mutton, with the choicest natural ingredients. Every kebab package that reaches you is processed and checked for quality and hygiene.

FAQs
  • How is Licious Kebab sourced?
    Licious Kebab is a premium produce that ensures you enjoy quality and fresh meat. We work with local farmers and the chicken is raised in a natural environment on controlled farms.
  • Why should I choose Licious Kebab?
    We strive to bring you only the best! We are extremely meticulous about the meat we put on your plate. For this reason, our chicken produce is handpicked. We believe that chicken tastes best when fresh and it truly uplifts the taste of your meals. Moreover, chicken from Licious is free from antibiotic residue and artificially injected hormones.
  • How long can I store kebabs in the refrigerator?
    Kebabs is best consumed fresh or on the day of its delivery, as it adds the best flavours to your dish. We do not recommend using frozen chicken, as it affects the taste, texture and nutritional properties of the meat. However, the meat, whether whole or cut, can be stored in a refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.
  • At what temperature must chicken be stored?
    The chicken must be stored in a refrigerator that is set at a temperature between 0 to 5 degrees. This is the same temperature that we maintain for the meat – from the time of procurement to the point of delivery.
